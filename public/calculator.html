What does being late actually cost?

Most teams have a rough sense that delay is expensive. Almost none have a number. This tool builds that number from two inputs: what the project delivers per month in revenue, margin, or cost savings — and when it reaches full run rate.

With that number on the table, finance decisions get sharper, mitigation spending gets justified, and the engineers who know where the real risks are stop being afraid to say so. Are you spending enough, right now, to protect against what being late would actually cost?
